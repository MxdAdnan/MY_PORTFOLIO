About Me


Hello, I'm Mohammed Adnan – a Digital Marketing Specialist with a creative heart and a coder’s mind.

My core strength lies in digital marketing, where I’ve built hands-on expertise in SEO, performance marketing, and paid advertising. From keyword research to technical on-page optimization, I focus on ranking websites higher and making sure every click counts.

I regularly work with tools like Google Analytics GA4, Search Console, Tag Manager, UTM tracking, and SEO suites to fine-tune performance and visibility.

I also bring solid experience in Email Marketing, running campaigns through platforms like Mailchimp and Constant Contact, crafting compelling newsletters, automating workflows, and improving open and click-through rates through testing and segmentation.

When it comes to paid advertising, I’ve managed Meta, Google, and LinkedIn campaigns, optimizing for conversions, lowering CPC, and improving ROAS. Whether it's building funnels, crafting ad creatives, or segmenting the audience — I know how to move the needle.

Outside of that, I enjoy creating websites using WordPress, adding custom tweaks using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I’ve worked across industries like e-commerce, livestock, and fashion, both in-house and freelance.

I’ve also built engaging Instagram Reels, interactive landing pages, and digital content that not only looks good but performs even better.

I believe in data-driven storytelling, backed by sharp strategy and clean execution. Adaptability is my strength, learning is my constant. This portfolio is a reflection of that blend – where marketing meets tech, and ideas turn into action.
